# My Research Interests and Summary Structure

## Interests
I am interested in:
- Agentic AI systems and multi-agent workflows
- Large language model applications and fine-tuning
- Computer vision and multimodal learning
- Reinforcement learning and optimization
- Neural architecture search and model efficiency

## Summary Structure
For each relevant paper, please provide:
1. Key findings and contributions
2. Methodology overview
3. Experimental results (if applicable)
4. Relevance to my interests
5. Potential applications or follow-up directions
6. Critical limitations or concerns

## Focus Areas
Pay special attention to papers that:
- Present novel agentic architectures or workflows
- Demonstrate practical applications of LLMs
- Introduce new techniques for model efficiency
- Combine multiple modalities (text, vision, etc.)
- Show strong empirical results
